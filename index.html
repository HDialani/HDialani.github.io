About Me

I am Hitesh Dialani, and I hold a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Power Electronics and Motors. I completed both my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees at the Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science (EEMCS) faculty of Delft University of Technology. Currently, I work as a Research and Education Technician at the Electrical Sustainable Power (ESP) Lab within the DC Systems, Energy Conversion & Storage (DCES) department. During my studies, I was a member of the Formula Student Team Delft, contributing to the DUT21 generation.
